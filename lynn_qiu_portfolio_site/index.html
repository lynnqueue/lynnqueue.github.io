About

Hello, I'm Lynn.

I am a Software Engineer from App Academy. As an account manager turned software engineer, I love making connections with people with the help of technology.

Prior to being a part of the tech world, I was an account manager in the fashion industry, the center of which is all about creativity and connecting with people. Through teaching myself some basic HTML, CSS, and JavaScript on the job, I discovered my passionate for technology.

I enjoy learning pratical tech skills and putting them to use for designing and building practical products. I am excited to embark on this journey of never-ending learning opportunities and challenges.
